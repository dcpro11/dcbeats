The Process

How it works.

01

Send Your Files

Share your stems, session files or raw recordings via a download link (WeTransfer, Google Drive, etc.). Include a reference track if you have a sound in mind.

02

I Get to Work

Depending on complexity, turnaround is typically 2–5 business days. You'll receive a preview before the final files are delivered.

03

Revisions & Delivery

Two rounds of revisions are included. Once approved, you receive WAV and MP3 files optimised for streaming, radio and any other format you need.
